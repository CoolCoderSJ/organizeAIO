organizeAIO

Your all in one tool to organize the best event possible.

Get started

Advanced Attendee Management

Create and update attendee attributes, manage check-ins, and add an attendee manually all from a single page.

Customizable Registration Form

With over 10 question types ranging from short answer to checkboxes, you can ask a participant anything you need to and get the data you need. Questions you add will update instantly on your hackathon's public page.

Event Schedule

Keep your participants updated with a full event schedule. Participants will be able to see the when and where an event is happening through an event's public page.

Instant Email Blasts

Send email blasts to your participants instantly. With the email blasts tool, you can query attributes from your attendees' registrations, and send emails to specific addresses.

Pull Finances from HCB

Run your finances on HCB by Hack Club? organizeAIO will show you your account balance as well as details for each transaction so you can keep an eye on your finances while organizing your event.

Project Gallery

Let participants create their projects through the public page. Participants can enter any number of links for their project, and organizeAIO will list all of them under a modal for you. Also, enable and disable the public project gallery so participants can see other projects.

Judging Access

organizeAIO isn't just for organizers, it's for judges as well! Invite your judges through your settings page. Once invited, judges will only be able to see the judging page. Judges can submit scores/comments, and organizers/judges can see all project links, comments, and the total score.

Team Access

Organizing with a team? organizeAIO has you covered. You can invite your team members in the settings page, after which they'll see your event on their homepage.

Public Pages

Your hackathon has its own home at an organizeAIO subdomain. Here, participants will be able to find more details about your event. Participants can get the schedule and judging criteria, view the project gallery, and register for the event.
