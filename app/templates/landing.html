organizeAIO

Your all-in-one tool to organize the best hackathon possible.

Get Started Learn More


Advanced Attendee Management

Manage attendees easily with custom attributes, check-ins, and more.

Customizable Registration Form

With over 10 question types ranging from short answer to multiple choice, you can ask a participant anything you need to and get the data you need.

Event Schedule

Keep your participants updated with your full event schedule, accessible at the click of a button.

Finances w/ HCB

Running your money on HCB by Hack Club? organizeAIO can pull your finances and show them on your dashboard.

Project Gallery

Participants can enter any number of links for their project, and organizeAIO will list all of them under a modal for you. Also, enable and disable the public project gallery so participants can see other projects.

Judging

organizeAIO isn't just for organizers, it's for judges as well! Invite your judges through your settings page. Once invited, judges will only be able to see the judging page. Judges can submit scores/comments, and organizers/judges can see all project links, comments, and the total score.

Organize With a Team

Organizing with a team? organizeAIO has you covered. You can invite your team members in the settings page, after which they'll see your event on their homepage.

Public Event Page

Your hackathon has its own home at an organizeAIO subdomain. Here, participants will be able to find more details about your event. Participants can get the schedule and judging criteria, view the project gallery, and register for the event.
